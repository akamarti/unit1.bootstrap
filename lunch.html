LUNCH









Finally, it's lunch time! But which rules do I have to follow in order to not get in trouble? Let's go through them together!



- You must be lined up outside quietly

- Social distancing must be observed

- There shouldn't be more than 4 people sitting at a table

- All trays and all litter should be put in the waste

- You should come only on the time that you are asked to



These rules are absolutely to be followed in order to maintain a clean organization between classes.

In this way, we'll all be eating in the outlooks with no problem!
